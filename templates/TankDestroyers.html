{% elif name == 'StrvS1' %}

The development of an advanced tank for Swedish army was performed by Sven Berge. First drafts of the unique vehicle were developed in 1956. By 1961, the design of the new vehicle was finalized, and a wooden prototype was built. Later, the first Strv S1 prototype was built and used for testing of the new type of suspension, which allowed for vertical aiming of the rigidly mounted gun. The Strv S2 prototype was developed by the end of 1962. Both prototypes became the basis for the mass production of the Strv 103 tank.

The vehicle was developed on the basis of the Churchill tank. The cabin with the 3-inch gun was placed in the front. Initially, 100 vehicles were ordered. Later, the order was reduced to 24 vehicles. Eventually, a total of 50 vehicles were manufactured from 1941 through 1942. However, they never saw action. The Churchill Gun Carrier is a transitional Tank Destroyer that can only be researched through the Churchill I Tier V Heavy Tank, allowing players to move from the British heavy line to the British tank destroyer line. It has a very powerful 32-pounder anti-tank gun, one of the most powerful guns available in its tier, but is limited by its poor mobility and armor.
